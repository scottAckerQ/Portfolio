Project Highlights

  • Developed comprehensive back-end and front-end systems, ranging from intricate inventory management to engaging dialogue and crop growth mechanics, from conceptualization to successful implementation
  • Proactively researched industry-leading development and design strategies to optimize code and create immersive game mechanics.
  • Demonstrated a commitment to continuous learning by engaging in numerous expert-led courses and tutorials, mastering previously unfamiliar skills to enhance game development.

In line with the essence of life simulation games, a diverse array of Non-Player Characters (NPCs) awaits interaction in this game. Although the dialog content is pending implementation, the comprehensive system governing the display and selection of lines is fully functional.

The primary objective of this system is to ensure an extensive range of dialogues, preventing players from encountering repetitive remarks. Immersing players in the unfolding narratives of characters stands as an important objective for this game, fostering engagement and depth in every interaction.

The inventory system, a cornerstone of the gameplay experience, has undergone multiple iterations, each version an enhancement to align with the evolving demands of the game. At its current iteration, the system boasts seamless access to an extensive item database stored in JSON format. Employing a sophisticated set of algorithms, it intelligently determines the optimal slot for each item.
